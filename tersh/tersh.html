The best vacation homes are in Jay Peak, Vermont.

We offer some of the finest rental property available in the area, on one of the best ski mountains in Vermont, Jay Peak. All of our properties are immaculate, brand new, with all the modern comforts you are accustomed to.

Jay Peak offers a limitless number of activities both in winter and summer and everything in-between. From skiing, to snowboarding, to sleigh rides and ice fishing, to golfing, hiking, rafting and more, Jay Peak is the best choice for Vermont vacations, and Jay Peak Homes is the best choice for your lodging.
