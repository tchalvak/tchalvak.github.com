Illusions of the Unexplainable


        The elements of indescribable

Mysteries dwell in the realms

Yet unknown to man, unreachable

Though hidden beneath the helm


        Of arbitrary reality

there unearthed and

Here entombed


        By enduring perceptions

that will not bend

beneath new ideas inceptions

that yet may lend


        A new method

To the madness

Of the elements.
