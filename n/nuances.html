UNREAD


Nuances


        The tiny beings

That hold again

all meanings

of wild gains

within their

hands.


        That still have the power

To change reality

And find the unexplainable lower

Realms of antiquity

Are gone and below the

Earth.


        As fictitious thought is

greatfully entrenched

and combined with this

the mind entranced

for ages long

gone.
